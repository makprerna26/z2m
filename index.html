About Steve Jobs

Steve Jobs was an American entrepreneur, inventor, and business magnate. He was the co-founder, chairman, and CEO of Apple Inc. Jobs is widely recognized as a pioneer of the personal computer revolution.

Personal Details

  • Full Name: Steven Paul Jobs
  • Born: February 24, 1955
  • Died: October 5, 2011
  • Nationality: American
  • Company: Apple Inc., Pixar

Major Achievements

  • Co-founded Apple Inc. in 1976
  • Introduced the Macintosh computer
  • Revolutionized mobile phones with the iPhone
  • Led the creation of iPad and iPod
  • Turned Pixar into a world-class animation studio

Famous Quote

"Stay hungry, stay foolish."
